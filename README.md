# ✈️ Make & Fly

Make & Fly is a STEM learning initiative focused on teaching students the fundamentals of aeromodelling through hands-on making and flying activities.

The program encourages curiosity, creativity, and engineering thinking by allowing students to design, build, and fly simple aircraft such as chuck gliders.

Make & Fly is an aeromodelling STEM learning initiative that teaches students how to design, build, and fly gliders and simple aircraft.


## 🚀 Mission

To inspire the next generation of innovators by making aerospace learning simple, practical, and accessible for school students.

---

## 🛠 Programs

• Chuck Glider Workshop  
• Aeromodelling STEM Program  
• School Innovation Workshops  
• Hands-on Engineering Activities  

---

## 📚 What Students Learn

• Basics of Aerodynamics  
• Aircraft Design Principles  
• Hands-on Model Building  
• Flight Testing and Iteration  
• Problem Solving & Engineering Thinking
